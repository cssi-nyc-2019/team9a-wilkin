GAME OBJECTIVE:

YOU HAVE TO ESCAPE FROM A MONSTER BY ANSWERING QUESTIONS WHILE YOU TRY TO KEEP THE MOST HEARTS POSSIBLE...

HOW TO PLAY...

  1. You have to answer a question right so you can pass to the next block


  2. If you don't get the question right you will lose a heart


  3. if you lose all of the hearts, the monster catches you and the GAME is OVER
